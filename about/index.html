Our Journey

2013 The Genesis

A Vision is Born

What started as a small passion project quickly became a mission. SumanTV was founded to give a voice to regional stories that mainstream media often overlooked, beginning a grassroots revolution in digital broadcasting.

2017 Expansion

Scaling Regional Content

Recognizing the massive demand for vernacular content, SumanTV expanded into multiple regional languages, launching dedicated studios and bringing high-quality journalism directly to smartphones across India.

2021 Milestone

Crossing 100M Views

A historic moment for the network. SumanTV shattered regional viewership records, cementing its authority in digital news, entertainment, and lifestyle content, creating a homegrown franchise of loyal viewers.

2026 The Future

Global Digital Authority

Today, SumanTV operates as a massive digital conglomerate, utilizing cutting-edge studio technology to produce award-winning original content, remaining an unparalleled force in Indian digital media.
